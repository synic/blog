<p>In just the last month or so, there have been a few people in the #exaile
channel on freenode asking if Exaile works in OS X. I honestly didn't know.</p>
<p>Today Lasse Riis confirmed that it does indeed work.
<a href='/media/images/macosx.jpg'>Here</a> is the first known screenshot of it
running on Apple's operating system. Lasse said that he will be posting
installation instructions on our Wiki soon.</p>
