That's right, it's been one year as of today.
<!-- end-summary -->

This time last year I was at home twiddling my thumbs waiting for something to
happen. I wasn't allowed to go to the hospital, and I couldn't think of anything
else. It was a very nerve-wracking experience. Up until that point, it hadn't
been entirely real; I'm not sure if I had accepted it yet. That day, though,
everything changed. All I wanted to do was be there, and it was tearing me up
that I couldn't go.

It's gotten a lot better, but only after months of fighting for it.

Happy birthday, Victoria! I'm glad you're with us!
